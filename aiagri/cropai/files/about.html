About The Project......

21st century is known as the age of Artificial Intelligence with AI technology developing for every field of science and commerce,there is
definitely a need to enhance the most basic and fundamental sector of human civilization, i.e. the agricultural sector.
India is an agricultural country hence there is much scope in using AI technology to improve quality of agricultural practices.

In agriculture there is a quick adaptation to AI in its various farming techniques. The concept of cognitive computing is the one which imitates human thought process as a model in computer. This results as turbulent technology in AI powered agriculture, rendering its service in interpreting, acquiring and reacting to different situations (based on the learning acquired) to enhance efficiency. To harvest benefits in the field by catching up with the recent advancements in farming sector, the farmers can be offered solutions via platforms like chatter-bot.

AI systems are also helping to improve harvest quality and accuracy what is known as precision agriculture. Precision agriculture uses AI technology to aid in detecting diseases in plants, pests, and poor plant nutrition on farms. AI sensors can detect and target weeds and then decide which herbicides to apply within the right buffer zone. This helps to prevent over application of herbicides and excessive toxins that find their way in our food.

Some Info About Group Project & Group

Our problem statement was to Develop an AI to help farmers in choosing right yield using weather prediction . According to our objectives We developed an A.I. using Time Series analysis to predict weather condition. Using ANN developing multi class classification for weather based crop prediction. From data we collect we feed this data to that AI module & module will predict right crop yield for that field.
